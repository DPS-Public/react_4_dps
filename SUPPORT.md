## 🚨 Critical Issues

If DPS becomes non-functional in a production or business-critical environment, please email:

**me@anarrustamov.com**

Suggested subject line:

`[URGENT] DPS production issue – [Organization Name]`

Please include:

- Environment details
- Issue summary
- Relevant logs or screenshots
- Steps to reproduce if known
- Commercial license or support agreement reference if applicable

Critical issue handling for non-licensed users remains best effort.
